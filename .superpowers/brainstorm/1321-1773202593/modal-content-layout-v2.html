What goes inside the case study modal?

Pick the content structure that fits how you want to tell your project stories

A

Minimal

Title · tags · 1 hero image · 2–3 paragraphs. Just enough to give context. Fast to write, focused. Works well if the images do the heavy lifting.

B

Structured Case Study

Metadata row (role, duration, type) → Problem → Process → Outcome. Standard UX portfolio format. Sections with text + images. Tells the full story in a familiar way recruiters expect.

C

Scroll Story

Long-form narrative with alternating text + wide images. Rich, editorial feel. Great for projects with strong visuals. Heaviest to produce — each project needs real writing and images.

my recommendation

Start with B — Structured Case Study. It's what recruiters and design leads expect, and the fixed sections (problem/process/outcome) give you a template to fill in rather than a blank page. You can always evolve toward C for specific showpiece projects later.
